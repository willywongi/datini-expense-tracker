Datini Expense Tracker

This webapp is born just to satisfy the need for me and my wife to collaboratively track expenses. We've been using Google Sheet with a Google Form for a couple of years but I just want to move out this little piece of my life from the all-encompassing embrace of the big G.

The project is opensource: you can find more info on getting the code or running an instance on your own server at the GitHub page.

The icon I'm using as a logo comes from: "stationery" by Azam Ishaq from the Noun Project.

The name I chose from this project is inspired by the book "The merchant of Prato" by Iris Origo. The book tells the story of Francesco di Marco Datini and how he mastered the art of keeping track of expenses.
